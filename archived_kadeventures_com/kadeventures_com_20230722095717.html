FAQ’S

Get answers to frequently asked questions about everything we do.

Is it ever too early to approach Kade about investing?

As a private equity company, we're interested across the board. In fact, most of our companies came to us when they were a couple of people and an idea, having raised no capital before they met us. So, even if you don't think you're ready, we'd still like to get to know you. Maybe we can even help in the meantime.

What if I've already raised some money from angels, is it too late to talk to you?

Of course not. While we're usually the first money in, we've worked with some teams that raised a small friends and family round before coming to us. That said, if you've already raised more than a few million dollars, we're probably not a fit.

Does Kade only invest in particular areas or industries?

No, While we embrace the unconventional, we were fortunate to invest in some of the most extraordinary brands.
So If you're building something different, we want to hear from you and learn about the vision of the future you have in mind.

What do you look for in a founding team?

We look for founders who bring the perfect blend of passion, expertise, and a touch of quirkiness. We want trailblazers who aren't afraid to challenge the status quo and dream big. So, if you've got the drive, the vision, and a sprinkle of quirkiness, we're ready to be your biggest cheerleaders!

Our team combines growth expertise with a sprinkle of adventure, creating a unique blend that sets us apart from the rest.
